How to Play

Keyboard
  • A / Left Arrow - move left
  • D / Right Arrow - move right
  • Space / W / Up - jump
  • P / Esc - pause
Controller
  • D-pad / Left Stick - move left or right
  • A button - jump
  • Start - pause / resume
  • B button - back (menus)
Touch
  • On-screen arrows - move left or right
  • Jump button or swipe up - jump
  • Tap canvas - jump
  • Swipe left / right - change lane

Collect coins - dodge cars, cones, trash cans, roadblocks - speed keeps increasing.
